Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about solar power

How long does a solar installation take?

Most installations take 1-3 days depending on the complexity of the system. Smaller residential systems can often be completed in just one day, while larger commercial installations may take 2-3 days.

What happens during rainy season or at night?

Your solar system stores excess energy in batteries during the day, which powers your business at night. Even on cloudy days, solar panels still generate 20-30% of their capacity.

How long do solar panels last?

Solar panels typically last 25-30 years, with most manufacturers offering 20-25 year performance warranties. Other system components have warranties ranging from 5-10 years.

Do I need permission to install solar panels?

For most residential and commercial installations, no special permission is required. We handle all necessary paperwork for larger commercial systems.

What maintenance is required?

Solar systems require minimal maintenance - occasional cleaning of panels and routine system checks. We offer maintenance packages for complete peace of mind.
